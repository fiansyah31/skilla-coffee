OUR STORY BEGIN AFTER COFFEE

NOT JUST ANOTHER COFFEE

In the region where I grew up, a large coffee plantation had been settled for years. I used to play there, along with my childhood peers. During that period, many times we “stole” coffee beans, picking them right from the branches. For me, it wasn’t just for fun. I had a kind of curiousity and was amazed by the fact that coffee farm could give many economic benefits to locals.
